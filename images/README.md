# Images Setup Instructions

Please save the following images to this folder with these exact names:

1. **logo.png** - The "Nails by Carolina" logo with the hand and leaf design
2. **professional1.jpg** - Photo of Carolina at desk with certifications on wall
3. **professional2.jpg** - Another professional photo of Carolina at desk
4. **salon-entrance.jpg** - Photo of Carolina at MY SALON Suites entrance
5. **opi-products.jpg** - Photo of OPI gel polish bottles on shelf

## How to add images:

1. Drag and drop the images from the flyer into this `images` folder
2. Rename them to match the names above
3. Refresh your browser at http://localhost:8000

The page will automatically display the images once they're in place!
